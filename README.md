# HR_Analysis_Dashboard

HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition & Retention Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition trends and identifying the key factors contributing to workforce turnover. By leveraging SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, the project provides actionable insights to support HR teams in improving employee retention, work satisfaction, and organizational productivity.

Project Objectives

⦁	Analyze employee retention and attrition across departments and job roles
⦁	Identify the major drivers behind employee turnover
⦁	Measure job satisfaction, income levels, working tenure, and promotions
⦁	Provide data-driven recommendations to strengthen employee engagement and organizational policies


Tools & Technologies Used
SQL:- Data cleaning, transformation & KPI calculations
Excel:-Preliminary analysis & dashboard mock-up
Power BI:- Final interactive HR analytics dashboard
Tableau:- Additional data visualizations & comparative analysis

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
⦁	Overall and department-wise attrition rate
⦁	Average hourly rate of male research scientists
⦁	Attrition rate vs monthly income range
⦁	Average working years across departments
⦁	Job role vs work-life balance
⦁	Attrition rate vs years since last promotion

Process Workflow
⦁	Imported and cleaned raw HR dataset
⦁	Performed data transformations using SQL
⦁	Calculated KPIs and statistical metrics
⦁	Designed interactive dashboards in Excel, Power BI, and Tableau
⦁	Interpreted insights and created recommendations for HR teams



Key Insights
⦁	Higher attrition observed in low-income groups and delayed promotions
⦁	Certain job roles show lower work–life balance, increasing turnover risk
⦁	Income and workload imbalance strongly impact employee satisfaction
⦁	Retention strategies should focus on workload distribution and timely rewards

Outcome

The HR analytics system helps organizations:
⦁	Detect workforce attrition patterns earlier
⦁	Improve employee engagement and satisfaction
⦁	Strengthen promotion and compensation processes
⦁	Reduce attrition and improve overall productivity
